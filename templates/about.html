About 'My mommy & me' blog

'My mommy & me' blog is the achievement of a community united in the search for quality time with their children. Through creativity, patience and mutual support we can carry out a different activity for a special day. The little ones will appreciate it infinitely and the parents will be able to test the capabilities of their little ones.

How can you participate?

  • You can show support by liking or commenting on other users' recipes.
  • You can post your own recipes.
  • You can reply to other users' comments.
  • Remember to be nice to other users and in their comments.

Before you participate, please login or you can sign up.
